Biography

I am Lok Raj Joshi, PhD student at Dr. Diel’s Lab, Department of Veterinary and Biomedical Sciecne, SDSU. I am working on viral pathogensis,viral diagnostic assay and vectored-vaccine developement. I have deep interest in next-generation sequencing and genomic data analysis. I use R and Linux command line tools for genomic data analysis.
